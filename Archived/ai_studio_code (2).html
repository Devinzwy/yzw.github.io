Research Highlights 研究重点

Nanomaterials

Controllable Synthesis of 2D Heterostructures 二维异质结的可控合成

Developed a novel CVD method to achieve vertical stacking of different 2D materials with atomic precision. This work opens new avenues for high-performance optoelectronic devices. 开发了一种新型 CVD 方法，实现了不同二维材料原子精度的垂直堆叠。这项工作为高性能光电器件开辟了新途径。

Energy Storage

Advanced Electrodes for Batteries 先进电池电极材料

Design of porous carbon frameworks derived from MOFs to enhance the ion transport kinetics in lithium-sulfur batteries, significantly improving cycle life. 设计源自 MOFs 的多孔碳骨架，以增强锂硫电池中的离子传输动力学，显著提高了循环寿命。
