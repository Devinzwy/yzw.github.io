Research Highlights 研究重点

Synthesis

Scalable Growth of 2D Monolayers 大面积二维单晶生长

Developing novel CVD strategies to achieve wafer-scale, high-mobility semiconductor monolayers. Our method significantly reduces grain boundaries and defects. 开发新型 CVD 策略以实现晶圆级、高迁移率的半导体单分子层生长。我们的方法显著减少了晶界和缺陷，为下一代电子器件奠定了基础。

Device Application

Flexible Nano-electronics 柔性纳电子器件

Integrating 2D materials into flexible substrates to create wearable sensors with high sensitivity. We explore the mechanical durability under extreme strain. 将二维材料集成到柔性基底上，制造具有高灵敏度的可穿戴传感器。我们主要研究器件在极端应变下的机械耐久性和光电响应。
