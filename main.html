Research Highlights 研究重点

Advanced Manufacturing

Scalable Nanomanufacturing of 2D Nanoribbons Via Controlled Fracture 通过可控断裂实现二维纳米带的批量制造

Pioneered a mechanical fracture method to achieve the scalable assembly of nanoribbons from diverse 2D materials with uniform width and spacing under ambient conditions (International Patent #WO2025015336A1). This technique provides a pathway for mass-producing high-density logic circuits, ultrasensitive gas sensors, and interconnects for next-generation nano-electronics. 研发了一种机械断裂方法，实现了在常温常压下从多种二维材料中批量组装具有均匀宽度和间距的纳米带（国际专利号 #WO2025015336A1）。该技术为大规模生产高密度逻辑电路、超灵敏气体传感器和下一代纳米电子器件的互连提供了途径。

Nanomechanics & Strain Engineering

Strain Engineering of 2D Semiconductors 二维半导体的应变工程

Elucidated the directionally resolved strain-coupled phononic properties of monolayer MoTe2 via in situ Raman spectroscopy to determine critical Grüneisen parameters. These findings establish a fundamental framework for tuning optoelectronic properties in flexible electronics and designing robust straintronic devices. 通过原位拉曼光谱阐明了单层 MoTe2 的方向分辨应变耦合声子特性，以确定关键的 Grüneisen 参数。这些发现为在柔性电子器件中调节光电特性以及设计鲁棒的应变电子器件奠定了基础。
